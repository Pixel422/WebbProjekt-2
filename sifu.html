20 Minutes Till Dawn

Summary

20 Minutes Till Dawn is a roguelike game where you play as a group of survivors trying to escape a zombie apocalypse. The game is a top-down shooter where you have to survive as long as possible. The game is very fast-paced and you have to be quick on your feet to survive. The game is very fun and it is a great game to play with friends. The game is very easy to learn and it is a great game to play if you want to play a game with your friends.
